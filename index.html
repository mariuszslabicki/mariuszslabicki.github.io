Mariusz Slabicki

Wireless Networks Researcher

Nokia

I am a computer scientist, who is interested in many aspects of wireless communications. Right now I am working at Nokia, as a System Specification Engineer.

In 2017 I have obtained PhD in Computer Science for the thesis about reconfiguration procedures for radio parameters in LTE networks. As a Postdoc at Aalto University I have conducted some research in LoRa networks. Some time ago, I was also dealing with management procedures for Internet of Things devices and Wireless Sensors Networks.

I am open for research cooperation with individuals and institutions which are connected with wireless communication systems, 4G/5G, IoT and so on. If you see a possibility to do something together, feel free to contact me.

Interests

  • network protocols

Projects

FLoRa

FLoRa is a simulation framework for carrying out end-to-end simulations for LoRa networks. It is based on the OMNeT++ network simulator. I am using it in my LoRa networks research.
Code Website

PyLTEs

PyLTEs is a framework that allows to evaluate models of protocols/mechanisms/approach for LTE networks. I have used it in my PhD research.
